Welcome to TigerSnatch!

Before you start snatching for course spots, here’s a brief guide on how to use TigerSnatch.

Step 1: Search for a course by course department, number, or title.
Step 2: Toggle “Notify Me” switches to add or remove yourself from waitlists for full sections.

Step 3: Return to the dashboard to check out the waitlists you are on.

And that’s it! The next time a spot opens up in a section and we reach your position in the waitlist, you will be notified immediately via email. Five minutes later, you will be removed from this waitllilst and the next tiger will be notified.

Disclaimer: Note that Tigersnatch waitlists are independent from official Registrar waitlists. You might not be able to enroll in courses shown on TigerSnatch even if spots free up. Always check Course Offerings for course-specific enrollment info.
